### Current Quoting Process (Primary Focus)

- Walk me through a quote from first customer touch to signed order -- step by step

- Where does the quote get built today? Excel? NetSuite? Something else?
- How long does a typical quote take from request to delivery?
- What are the most common reasons a quote has to be reworked or revised?
- How do repeat customers get handled differently from new ones? (They mentioned ~70% retention)

### SKU and Product Data

- How many SKUs are we working with? Ballpark?
