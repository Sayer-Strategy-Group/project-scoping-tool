# STRIVE - Sales & HubSpot Discovery Session

**Prepared by:** Kyle Harbuck, Harbuck Consulting
**Date:** February 24, 2026
**Participants:** Andrea Kassees (Director of Sales & Strategic Partnerships, STRIVE), Jodi Gruber (CEO, STRIVE), Kyle Harbuck

---

## Purpose

This discovery session is designed to understand STRIVE's current sales processes, HubSpot CRM usage, and reporting needs. The findings will directly inform a scoped proposal for HubSpot optimization, segmentation, and board-ready reporting ahead of the March 4 board meeting.

---

## Lead Questions (Start Here)

These are the highest-signal questions. Prioritize these if time is short.

1. **Walk me through the sales cycle from lead to close for a typical SMB deal vs. a broker pack deal.** What steps happen, who's involved, and where does it break down?

2. **What is the single most frustrating thing about HubSpot for you right now?** The thing that wastes the most time or causes the most headaches for the sales team.

3. **What data in HubSpot do you not trust today?** Where do you find yourself going to spreadsheets or gut feel instead of the CRM?

4. **How are you forecasting today?** Spreadsheet, HubSpot forecast tool, gut feel, or a combination?

5. **Given the deal size variability ($6K deals alongside $400K broker packs), how do you currently separate those in reporting?** Or is everything lumped together today?

6. **What are your top 3 priorities if you could wave a magic wand and fix anything in HubSpot tomorrow?**

---

## Sales Process Deep Dive

7. **How are deal stages currently set up in HubSpot?** Are the pipeline stages the same for all deal types (microsites, app deals, broker packs), or do you have separate pipelines?

8. **What's your lead source breakdown?** Inbound vs. outbound vs. broker referral -- and is that being tracked consistently in HubSpot today?

9. **When a broker buys a pack and resells, how do you track the downstream accounts?** Is that managed in HubSpot or somewhere else entirely?

10. **How do you differentiate between your sales motions today?** (SMB, mid-market, enterprise/broker packs) Is there a clear handoff or threshold between them?

11. **How does Divi fit into the sales motion?** Is it sold alongside the core STRIVE platform, bundled, or treated as a separate product line?

---

## HubSpot CRM - Current State

12. **Are there fields or properties the team is supposed to fill out but doesn't?** What's adoption like on data entry discipline across the team?

13. **What automations or workflows are currently running in HubSpot?** Do you know which ones are working well vs. which ones might be stale or broken?

14. **Are there any integrations between HubSpot and other tools you use?** (email, marketing platforms, billing, contract tools, etc.)

---

## Reporting & Board Visibility

15. **When Jodi or the board asks "how's the pipeline looking," what do you pull and how long does it take?** What report do you wish existed but doesn't?

16. **What metrics do you track weekly or monthly?** (e.g., deals created, win rate, average deal size, cycle length, activity metrics)

---

## Team Workflow & Tools

17. **What does your team's daily/weekly HubSpot usage actually look like?** Are reps living in it throughout the day or logging activity after the fact?

18. **What tools outside HubSpot does the sales team use day-to-day?** (LinkedIn, email sequences, proposal/contract tools, project management, etc.)

19. **What does the handoff look like after a deal closes?** Who owns implementation/onboarding, and does that process live in HubSpot or somewhere else?

20. **What does onboarding a new sales rep look like today?** Is there documentation on the sales process or is it mostly tribal knowledge?

---

## Future State

21. **What would "great" look like for you 90 days from now?** If we get this right, what changes for you and the team?
